Mathematics

~[if#chkGrade.~([Students]grade_level)=2] [else#chkGrade] [/if#chkGrade]
2 N1 Say the number sequence from 0 to 100 by: 2s, 5s and 10s, forward and backward, using starting points that are multiples of 2, 5 and 10 respectively; 10s using starting points from 1 to 9; and 2s starting from 1. ~[if#Y.~([Students.U_PEI_FL]Math_2_N1)$"Y"] ~([Students.U_PEI_FL]Math_2_N1) [else#Y] [/if#Y]
2 N4 Represent and describe numbers to 100, concretely, pictorially and symbolically. ~[if#Y.~([Students.U_PEI_FL]Math_2_N4)$"Y"] ~([Students.U_PEI_FL]Math_2_N4) [else#Y] [/if#Y]
2 N6 Estimate quantities to 100 using referents. ~[if#Y.~([Students.U_PEI_FL]Math_2_N6)$"Y"] ~([Students.U_PEI_FL]Math_2_N6) [else#Y] [/if#Y]
2 N9 Demonstrate an understanding of addition (limited to 1 and 2-digit numerals) with answers to 100 and the corresponding subtraction by: using personal strategies for adding and subtracting with and without the support of manipulatives; creating and solving problems that involve addition and subtraction; explaining that the order in which numbers are added does not affect the sum; and explaining that the order in which numbers are subtracted may affect the difference. ~[if#Y.~([Students.U_PEI_FL]Math_2_N9)$"Y"] ~([Students.U_PEI_FL]Math_2_N9) [else#Y] [/if#Y]
2 N10 Apply mental mathematics strategies, such as: using doubles; making 10; one more, one less; two more, two less; building on a known double; and addition for subtraction to determine basic addition facts to 18 and related subtraction facts. ~[if#Y.~([Students.U_PEI_FL]Math_2_N10)$"Y"] ~([Students.U_PEI_FL]Math_2_N10) [else#Y] [/if#Y]
2 PR2-RR2 Demonstrate an understanding of increasing patterns by: describing; reproducing; extending; and creating patterns using manipulatives, diagrams, sounds and actions (numbers to 100). ~[if#Y.~([Students.U_PEI_FL]Math_2_PR2_RR2)$"Y"] ~([Students.U_PEI_FL]Math_2_PR2_RR2) [else#Y] [/if#Y]
2 PR4-RR4 Record equalities and inequalities symbolically, using the equal symbol or the not equal symbol. ~[if#Y.~([Students.U_PEI_FL]Math_2_PR4_RR4)$"Y"] ~([Students.U_PEI_FL]Math_2_PR4_RR4) [else#Y] [/if#Y]
